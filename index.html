Our Program





A program to provide advanced digital training and experience for North Lawndale Restorative Justice Community Court participants with courses to be conducted at Mount Sinai Hospital System at 1500 S Fairfield Ave, Chicago, IL

Curriculum




Participants will be guided through a variety of computer science disciplines to prepare them for lucrative positions in the data processing field or to equip them with the digital technologies necessary for success in any modern business venture, with emphasis on consulting and freelancing activities.
